Use Cases

With our broad expertise, our products support diverse clients. These are some cases we have worked with in the past.

Medical Image Analysis

We build solutions which analyze data of your clients automatically to see whether an anomaly is present or not. So far we have trained our algorithms on cancer, tumor and dental X-ray images. Since we are building the software into your premises, data protection is given by default.

Integrability & Data Protection

Our solution can be implemented on your premises and only for a given group of employees or for anyone in your enterprise. You decide.

Voice Assistant or Chat Bot

With our multi-purpose platform TextSpace, we can easily and quickly create your voice assistant or chat bot for your customers. It can be integrated into your website or product and assists your customers whenever they want to use it. For example, it can help them choosing the right movie, providing them with background information to a given movie or order tickets.
